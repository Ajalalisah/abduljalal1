HOW TO USE ORDERLIST AND UNORDERLIST

yadda ake orderlist ko unorder list

awajan rubutu mutun kan iya rubutagerin abu da order list ko unorderlist misali.

yadda xakayi rubutu da orderlist

  1. abduljalal
  2. isah
  3. yahaya
  4. ibrahim

yaddaxakayi rubutu da unorderlist

  • fateemah
  • naseer
  • adam
  • umar

ana iya saka unorderlist acikin orderlist ko orderlist acikin unorderlist misali.

yadda xakasa unorderlist acikinorderlist

  1. aisha
  2. hauwa'u
  3. khadeeja
  4. hussaina
    • abubakar
    • ameera
    • amina
    • hassana

ga kuma yaddaxakasaorderlist acikin unorderlist

  • haleemah
  • maryam
  • xainab
  • aseeya
    1. muhammad
    2. abdullahi
    3. ahmad
